ABOUT US

Royal Jelly Events is a Barcelona-based event collective that curates live music, DJ performances, bachata nights, and art-driven experiences in some of the city's most unique spaces and private venues — from intimate flamenco shows in restaurants to vibrant DJ sets in clubs, pop-ups, and cultural spaces.

Founded with a passion for creativity and connection, Royal Jelly Events blends local authenticity with international energy, bringing together talented musicians, DJs, and artists to craft events that feel real, warm, and unforgettable.

More than event organizers, we are community builders, connecting people through the universal language of music, dance, and art.
